Create a **portfolio Case Study page template for a Product Designer**.

The layout must be designed for a portfolio website and optimized for **HR recruiters, Design Directors, and Product Designers** reviewing the case quickly.

Design a **clean, modern, minimal layout** similar to product design portfolios used by senior designers in fintech and SaaS companies.

The page should be structured to clearly show **problem → process → solution → impact**.

Use large typography, clear hierarchy, and plenty of white space.
Sections should be visually separated and easy to scan.

Include the following sections in this order:

1. **Case Study Hero**

* Project title
* Short one-sentence description of the project
* Key impact metrics (e.g. +34% conversion, ↓2x drop-off)
* Project meta information:

  * Role
  * Team
  * Timeline
  * Tools

2. **Overview**

* Short paragraph explaining the product and context
* Screenshot of the product

3. **Problem**

* Description of the main user and business problem
* Optional funnel or analytics visualization

4. **Goals & Success Metrics**

* Clear project goals
* Metrics used to measure success

5. **Research**

* Methods used (interviews, analytics, testing)
* Key insights discovered

6. **Problem Definition**

* Clear “How might we” statement defining the design challenge

7. **Exploration**

* Early ideas, sketches, flows, or alternative concepts
* Show design thinking and trade-offs

8. **Solution**

* Final UX flow
* Key UI screens
* Explanation of important design decisions

9. **Testing**

* How the solution was validated
* Usability testing or prototype testing insights

10. **Results / Impact**

* Quantitative metrics
* Before vs After comparison
* Business impact

11. **Learnings**

* Key lessons from the project
* Design insights gained

12. **Next Steps**

* Future improvements or opportunities

Design guidelines:

* Use a **12 column grid**
* Clear spacing between sections
* Large case study headings
* Visual storytelling with images and diagrams
* Emphasize metrics and impact
* Optimize for **fast scanning by recruiters**

The result should look like a **high-quality case study template used by senior product designers** in companies like Stripe, Linear, or Airbnb.
